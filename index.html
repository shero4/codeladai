Why Sol.ai?

Sol.Ai’s lenses are built to display a variety of things ranging from Time and Weather to phone calls and texts. With a few simple gestures, you can even loop Old Town Road till your ears fall off. Can’t exactly wear glasses without ears can you?
Give yourself Sol.Ai.

What's Sol.ai?

Imagine the world as your display, and the buttons: your fingertips. Sol.Ai are a pair of glasses unlike any other, allowing simple hand gestures like snapping, swiping , or even simply rubbing your fingers together to be detected with insane precision. You can even simply use them as a pair of shades. (We promise you won’t look like a nerd).
